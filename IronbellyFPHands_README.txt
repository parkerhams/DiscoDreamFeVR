ADD TO PROJECT
Click the Add to Project button in the Epic Launcher to add this pack to your project. You must have version 4.13 or higher installed.

ANIMATIONS
To demo the animations, click the Play button in Unreal.
Punch - Left Click
Push Door - Right Click
Walk - W
Sprint - Hold W + Shift
Jump - Space
Grab Low - G
Grab High - H
Throw - T

CHANGE FP HANDS & MATERIALS
1) In your Content Browser, go to Content > FirstPersonBPIronbelly > Blueprints
2) Open the FirstPersonCharacterIronbelly Blueprint
3) Click on the Viewport tab
4) Click on the FP hand mesh, these will be in idle animation mode
5) Go to the Details tab > Mesh and select a new Skeletal Mesh from the dropdown
6) Under the Materials dropdown, select a new Element from the dropdown
7) Close the Blueprint
8) Click the play button to view newly selected hands and materials
9) Enjoy!

SEPARATE / VR HAND MESHES
Our demo utilizes one skeletal rig with both hands. If your project requires separate left and right hand meshes, we've provided separate meshes in Content > IBFPSHands > Hands > SeparatedHands. If your working on a VR project, we've provided separate hand meshes specific for VR in Content > IBFPSHands > Hands_VR.
